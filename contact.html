Common Questions
FAQ
How far in advance should I book?

For campaign shoots, 2–4 weeks advance booking is ideal. For larger productions with multiple team members, 4–6 weeks gives us time to do the project justice. Rush bookings can sometimes be accommodated — just ask.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes. We're Warsaw-based but regularly work across Poland and Europe. Travel costs are factored into the project quote. If you have something international in mind, let's talk.

Can we negotiate the rate?

Our rates reflect the quality and craft we deliver. That said, we're always open to a conversation — especially for long-term partnerships or interesting creative projects. Note that negotiating the rate means adjusting the deliverables, not the quality.

Do you provide RAW files?

RAW files are available as an add-on, priced separately from the standard package. Think of them as licensing the full creative potential of the shoot. We'll discuss this during the briefing process.

What's included in the brief process?

Every project starts with a discovery conversation where we understand your brand, goals, and vision. From there we develop a creative concept, moodboard, and production plan before anything is shot.

Do you work with new or emerging brands?

Absolutely. Some of our most exciting work has been with brands in their early stages. If the creative vision is strong and the commitment is real, we're in — regardless of the size of the brand.
