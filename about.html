The Beginning

Built from a belief that every brand has a story worth telling.

Anansé Creative Haus was founded by Clifford (Cliffson) — a Ghanaian director of photography, creative director, and marketing specialist based in Warsaw, Poland.

The name Anansé comes from Anansi — the spider of West African and Caribbean folklore, known as the keeper of stories. A trickster. A weaver. A world-builder. That energy is woven into everything we do.

We started as a photography studio and quickly evolved into a full production house — one that doesn't just capture moments, but builds complete visual worlds for brands. From editorial campaigns to cinematic brand films, we approach every project with the same obsession: tell a story that matters.

Warsaw-based. Globally minded. And only getting started.
