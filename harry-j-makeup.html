The Project
A beauty editorial built around the art of transformation.
Editorial Beauty Fashion Warsaw

In collaboration with Harry J Make Up, we created a full editorial shoot that celebrates the craft of makeup artistry — raw, intentional, and deeply visual. Every frame was built to feel like a moment, not just a photograph.

Shot under studio lighting with a controlled palette, this project blends fashion editorial sensibility with beauty campaign precision. The result is a body of work that positions Harry J Make Up at the intersection of art and craft.
