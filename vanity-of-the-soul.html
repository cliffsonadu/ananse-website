The Project
Heritage turned into visuals that connect.
Brand Campaign Editorial Vintage Warsaw

For Vanity of the Soul — a Warsaw-based vintage bag store — we didn't just shoot products. We built a world around them. Every frame was crafted to transform timeless vintage pieces into experiences, turning heritage into visuals that connect.

This campaign blends editorial storytelling with commercial precision, positioning Vanity of the Soul as more than a store — but a destination for those who understand that objects carry stories.
